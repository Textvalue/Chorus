Last quarter one of my reps closed a €40k deal. It didn't start with a cold email. It started with a comment. She'd been replying to the VP's posts for three weeks — nothing salesy, just useful. So when he finally had a problem we solve, she wasn't a stranger in his inbox. She was the person who clearly got it. We track this now. Deals that start with engagement close about twice as fast as deals that start cold. The company page didn't do that. A person did. Founders: are your reps building that kind of presence, or just sending sequences?
